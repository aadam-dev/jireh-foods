# Jireh Natural Foods – Branding

## Media assets

- **Location:** `docs/jireh-media/` (source); **`public/jireh/`** (used by the site).
- **Logo:** Add `logo.png` or `logo.svg` to `public/jireh/`. Used in the top bar and hero; text fallback if missing.
- **Hero:** Add `hero.jpg` to `public/jireh/` for the hero background. Fallback: stock food image.
- **Food photos:** Optional `food1.jpg`, `food2.jpg`, etc. in `public/jireh/` for future gallery or menu use.

See [jireh-media/README.md](jireh-media/README.md) for details.

## Colours (theme)

Current accent colours are in `app/globals.css` (`:root`):

- `--accent`: `#c45c26`
- `--accent-hover`: `#a34a1e`
- `--background`: `#faf8f5`
- `--foreground`: `#2c2419`
- `--muted`: `#6b5b4f`

To match Jireh brand guidelines, replace these hex values with your palette.
