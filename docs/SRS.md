# SRS: Jireh Natural Foods

**Industry:** Natural foods restaurant / grill  
**Goal:** Modern, high-converting website that drives visits, calls, directions, and Bolt Food orders.  
**Reusable for:** Any small restaurant or eatery with a focused menu and delivery partners.

---

## 1. Project overview

- **Purpose:** Establish strong online presence; showcase ambience and menu; convert visitors to diners (call, directions, reserve).
- **Target users:** Local diners, families, expats, tourists searching for restaurants in Accra.
- **Success metrics:** Click-to-call, Get directions, contact/form submits, time on site.

---

## 2. Brand and positioning

- **Tone:** Warm, inviting, premium but approachable.
- **Colours:** Rich neutrals (deep brown, cream), accent (e.g. terracotta or gold) for CTAs.
- **Typography:** Clear heading font (e.g. serif or rounded sans), readable body (sans). Scale: hero 2.5–3rem, body 1rem, captions 0.875rem.

---

## 3. Site structure

- **Home** (single-page or multi: Hero, About, Menu, Hours & location, Contact).
- **Optional:** Menu (dedicated page or section), Reservations CTA.

---

## 4. Page-by-page requirements

### Hero
- Full-viewport hero; headline: business name + one strong line (e.g. "Where great food meets great company").
- Subhead: short tagline or "Accra's favourite spot for…".
- Primary CTA: "Call now" / "Get directions" / "Reserve a table".
- Background: high-quality image (dining area or signature dish). Optional: subtle overlay for text contrast.
- **UI:** One clear CTA; trust badge area for rating (e.g. "4.3 ★ · 3,114+ reviews").

### Sections
- **About:** 2–3 sentences on story, vibe, what makes the place special.
- **Menu / Offerings:** Section or link to menu (categories or highlights). Placeholder: "Full menu available on request / PDF."
- **Hours & location:** Prominent hours (Mon–Sun 11 am–8 pm from CSV). Address, embedded map or "Open in Google Maps" button.
- **Contact:** Phone (click-to-call), optional contact form; Google Maps link.
- **Footer:** Address, phone, social (Facebook: Livingroomghana), hours summary, copyright.

---

## 5. UI/UX and visual (bargaining power)

- **Layout:** Clean grid, generous whitespace, mobile-first (stack on small screens).
- **Animations:** Scroll-triggered fade/slide for sections; hover lift/shadow on cards and buttons; optional staggered list; smooth scroll.
- **Trust:** Prominent "4.3 ★ · 3,114+ reviews"; "Open now" or next opening time if applicable; sticky CTA bar (Call / Directions) on mobile.
- **Accessibility:** Contrast ≥4.5:1, focus states, semantic HTML, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected.
